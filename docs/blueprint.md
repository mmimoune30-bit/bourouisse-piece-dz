# **App Name**: AutoPièces DZ

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Secure registration and login functionality for both sellers and customers to manage their accounts.
- Seller Product Listing: Sellers can add, edit, and remove auto parts, including detailed descriptions, pricing, images, and inventory levels.
- Product Catalog Browsing: Customers can browse through a catalog of auto parts, filter by categories, make searches, and view listed products.
- Product Details Page: Detailed view for each auto part, displaying specifications, multiple images, and seller information.
- AI-Powered Search Tool: A generative AI tool that suggests relevant search terms or related products based on user input, improving discovery.
- Seller Dashboard: A dedicated interface for sellers to manage their listings, monitor inventory, and track sales data.
- Simple Order Placement: Customers can select auto parts and proceed through a streamlined process to place an order.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, professional indigo (#1F1F66) to convey reliability and professionalism, suitable for main branding elements and calls to action.
- Background color: A very light, subtle periwinkle (#EEEEF8) to ensure high readability and a clean interface, enhancing content clarity.
- Accent color: A vibrant sky blue (#33CCFF) to draw attention to interactive elements, alerts, and key information, providing a dynamic contrast.
- Unified font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for both headlines and body text, chosen for its modern, neutral, and highly readable characteristics, perfect for displaying product information clearly across all devices.
- Utilize clean, crisp line-art icons that complement the professional aesthetic and ensure easy recognition for different functionalities like search, cart, and seller management.
- Implement a clear, modular grid-based layout for product listings and dashboards, ensuring a highly organized and scalable user interface across various screen sizes.
- Subtle and functional micro-animations, such as hover effects on product cards and loading indicators, to provide engaging user feedback without distracting from the primary content.
